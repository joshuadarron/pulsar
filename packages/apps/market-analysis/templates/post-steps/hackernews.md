1. Sign in to https://news.ycombinator.com.
2. Open the submit form at https://news.ycombinator.com/submit.
3. Title: {{title}} (under 80 characters; HN truncates).
4. URL: leave blank for a text post, or paste the source URL the draft is responding to.
5. Text: paste the body of the draft (only used when URL is blank).
6. Submit. Hacker News has no scheduling, so post when the audience is awake (US morning typically performs best).
