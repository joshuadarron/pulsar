2. Paste the draft body. LinkedIn strips most markdown, so review formatting once pasted.
3. Add 3 to 5 hashtags by hand at the bottom. Pick tags relevant to the angle and your audience; do not paste a generic tag list.
4. Tag relevant accounts (companies, people the post references) using @ mentions where it adds context.
5. Add a media attachment if it strengthens the angle (image, document, or short video). Leave blank otherwise.
6. Schedule: {{schedule_time}} (use LinkedIn's native scheduler, the clock icon next to "Post").
7. Review, then schedule or post.
